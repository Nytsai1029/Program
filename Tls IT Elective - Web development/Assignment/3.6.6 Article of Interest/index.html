Lenses: Seeing Small and Seeing Far

And Just What Is A Lens?

A lens is a curved surface that bends light to a focal point. People have been using lenses since ancient times to help their eyes.

Microscopes

A microscope lets you see tiny things that are too small for your eyes alone. It uses lenses to magnify the details so you can study them up close.

Telescopes

A telescope helps you see things that are far away in space. It gathers light from stars and planets so you can view them from Earth.
